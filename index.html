International Seminar Digital Media in Challenging Era, Technology, Communicationand Frontier.

Objective

In an era where technological progress occurs rapidly, The dynamic relationship between technology and mass communication and the scope of digital media also happens and changes quickly, especially in the present era. Challenges of digital media The interplay of technology, communication, and advanced thinking. This context is essential for many reasons, including technological advancements, communication strategy, innovation and creativity, globalization considerations, cultural differences, and data-driven decision-making. Therefore, the combination of technology, communication, and advanced thinking is considered something that must be prepared to face challenges in the digital media era. Helping content creators and digital media professionals innovate, connect with various stakeholders, and adapt to the ever-changing landscape. Consequently, the School of Informatics has joined with the Informatics Innovation Center of Excellence to organize an academic seminar on topics that are the faculty's highlights. which includes information technology, mass communication, and digital content. It is also a project in collaboration with the faculty's research network, including the University of Amsterdam, UNTAG University Surabaya, Xavier University, and Tunku Abdul Rahman University of Management and Technology. The project's target audience is teachers, academics, researchers, and students in Thailand and internationally. The seminar aims to allow participants to learn about the latest research findings, trends, and developments in the field. Networking with experts from other institutions both in Thailand and abroad, as well as increasing knowledge and skills in the field of information science To prepare for this era of change.
